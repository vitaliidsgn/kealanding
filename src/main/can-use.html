An overview of the technology

  • Fact-checking &
    anti-hallucination

    Content quality and factual accuracy are our top priorities. To ensure accuracy and trust, we built a multi-layered fact-checking and anti-hallucination system. Every generated course is filtered through a combination of structured data sources, cross-referenced claims, and real-time validation models, designed to minimize errors and deliver learning you can count on.

  • Context-aware conversational AI

    The interactive Q&A is powered by a context-aware conversational engine that understands not just your question, but also the lecture you’re listening to, the topic at hand, and your overall learning journey. Unlike generic AI chat, Sun’s responses are grounded in the specific course content, ensuring every answer is relevant, coherent, and personalized. It’s a guided dialogue, purpose-built for meaningful learning.

  • Profile & request-based hyper-personalization

    Creating a hyper-personalized content experience is at the core of our product. Whether you're selecting the topic, choosing a preferred voice, or setting the course length, every aspect of your journey is tailored to you. Sun intelligently blends your preferences, learning history, and real-time input to generate courses that feel truly custom, designed around who you are and what you want to learn.
